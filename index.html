Tour Packages

Bike Monkey Tours offers a range of bicycle touring packages for riders of all levels of fitness. All tours are supported by a van carrying your personal belongings, food and water, tools, and first aid supplies. Accommodation is in hotels and guesthouses especially chosen for their local charm and the high quality of amenities and services they offer. Our guides are native English speakers, fluent in Chinese, and experienced at leading groups of cyclists in Taiwan.

Central Taiwan

In Central Taiwan, you'll find Jade Mountain, the island's tallest peak, Alishan Forest Recreation Area, home to giant trees, and the resort of Sun Moon Lake. Contact Bike Monkey Tours to learn about planning a custom tour to any or all of the amazing locations. Guided hiking tours can be arranged with locally-based, foreign-run tour operators.

Southern Taiwan

Hengchun Peninsula lies at the southern tip of the Taiwan. It's home to Kenting National Park, Taiwan's top beach resort town. Combine a visit to this area with a tour of the East Coast or Central Taiwan.

Why Taiwan?

Subtropical Taiwan is an ideal bicycle touring destination. Ride along unspoiled coasts, through sheltered valleys, among fields of golden rice, up one of the most spectacular marble gorges on earth, and test your strength against the 90-kilometer climb to Wuling Summit, at 3,275 meters above sea level.

Taiwan is easily reached from the US or Europe by most major airlines. Visitor visas are available at the airport for travelers from most countries. Take the Airport Metro into Taipei and enjoy the city's historic districts and vibrant night markets to sample some of foods that make Taipei one of Asia's dining capitals.
